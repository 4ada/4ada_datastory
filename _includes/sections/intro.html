Introduction

COVID-19 is one of the biggest events in the 21st century and it will go on forever in the annals of history. This virus has transformed our way of life and work, as well as how we interact with others and see the outside world. We are aware that each country handled the issue differently and responded with varying weights and measures: some governments, who were more concerned about the virus, imposed early mobility restrictions; other governments, who were less concerned, simply began issuing public warnings without taking further action. Particularly in the start of 2020, each decision had a significant impact on how COVID-19 spread and how many people became infected.
